Our 30-Year Journey

Founded by Bhisham and Leena Sainani, Archies began with a simple mission: to make reading affordable for everyone in Dubai. For decades, our spaces in Karama and Satwa have been a safe haven for book lovers.

We operate on a unique model. Buy a book and return it anytime for 50% cash back, or sign up for our flexible membership plans to rent up to 24 books a month!
