About Archies Library

Archies Library has been a trusted community bookshop and lending library in Dubai since 1994. With branches in Karama and Satwa, it offers used and new books, magazines, school books, fiction, business, children’s books and more.

The Karama branch is known as Archies Bookshop, while the Satwa branch operates as Book World.
